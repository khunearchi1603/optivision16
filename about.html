About OptiVision

At OptiVision, we believe everyone deserves to see clearly and look stylish doing it. Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable eyewear that combines fashion with function.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, OptiVision started as a small boutique in Pune, India. Inspired by the need for stylish yet affordable eyewear, our founders brought together a team of designers and optical experts to redefine how glasses are made and sold.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across the country through our online store and continue to innovate with every frame we design.

Our Mission

Affordability

We make designer-quality frames accessible to everyone.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials and packaging wherever possible.

Vision For All

We donate glasses to communities in need with every purchase.
